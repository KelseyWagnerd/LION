LION was created to provide a space for local professionals to connect with one another outside of their organization or school, to foster a community that can share ideas/opportunities/research in a psychologically safe environment, and to (eventually) become a resource for both organizations and professionals to seek out top talent and great opportunities.

Degree not required! LION is open to anyone with an interest in...

  • Psychology (Social, Org Health, I/O, HFAC, etc.),
  • Psychometrics,
  • Data Analytics/Analysis,
  • HR design,
  • Talent development,
  • Recruitment/Selection,
  • or Learning more.
